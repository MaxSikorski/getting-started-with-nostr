# Getting Started with Nostr

A plain-English guide to joining Nostr: understanding your keys, picking a client, protecting
your key with a signer, finding people, and sending your first zap. Written and maintained by
[Max Sikorski](https://github.com/MaxSikorski) for the Bitcoin & Nostr weekly meetup.

**Read it here:** [Getting Started with Nostr 101](https://maxsikorski.github.io/getting-started-with-nostr/)

## Works offline, on purpose

This guide is plain HTML with no build step, no tracking, and no external dependencies. To take
it with you:

1. Click the green **Code** button above
2. Choose **Download ZIP**
3. Unzip anywhere and open `index.html`

The whole guide works on any phone or computer with no internet connection.
